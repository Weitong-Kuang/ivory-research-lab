# Personality Consistency: Behavioral Modeling

This experiment aims to measure the consistency of a digital persona's personality across different conversational contexts.

## Methodology

We are using a multi-agent simulation to test how the digital persona responds to various emotional and intellectual stimuli.

## Current Status

We have achieved 85% consistency in conversational style, but we are still working on improving emotional depth.
